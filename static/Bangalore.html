Tourist Places and Things to do in Bangalore

Bangalore, the capital of Karnataka, has a lot more to it than just being the IT hub of India. It has the most amazing nightlife too with countless pubs and bars lined along the streets of Koramangala and Indiranagar. The night trotters can dance till late at places such as Monkey Bar and Toit, attend live music gigs at Hard Rock Café and The Humming Tree or stroll around the buzzing MG Road. There is no dearth of options for foodies as eateries and restaurants in Bangalore serve everything from local to international fare. Street food like pav bhaji can be best enjoyed at VV Puram Food Street.

There are some great places to visit in Bangalore, starting from Cubbon Park and Lalbagh Botanical Garden to Bangalore Palace, Vidhana Soudha, and other historical places. Besides sightseeing, this megacity has other fun things to do for visitors including taking a ride at Wonderla and shopping at Commercial Street. Adventure seekers might love to camp under the stars at Ramanagara, go for a trek to Savandurga Hill or take a road trip from Bangalore to hill stations like Ooty and Coorg.

A one-day trip to Bannerghatta National Park or weekend getaways from Bangalore to nearby tourist destinations such as Mysore and Hassan might be a great option for a relaxing vacation.
